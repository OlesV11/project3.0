F.A.Q.

  • What is the difference between natural and artificial fur?

    Natural fur is made from animal skins, offering insulation, durability, and natural texture. Artificial fur is made from synthetic materials, providing an ethical alternative available in various textures and colors.

  • How do I care for artificial fur?

    Artificial fur can be hand-washed or machine-washed on a gentle cycle using mild detergents. Avoid high temperatures and let it air dry naturally.

  • Does artificial fur cause allergies?

    Our artificial fur is made from hypoallergenic materials, making it safe even for people with sensitive skin.

  • How can I choose the right size?

    We provide a detailed size chart for each product. You can also contact our consultant for personalized assistance.

  • Do you provide a warranty for your products?

    Yes, we offer a warranty for all products, provided the care instructions in the product passport are followed.

  • How do I take care of natural fur products?

    Store the product in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid humidity, and if it gets wet, allow it to dry naturally. Regular cleaning by a specialized dry cleaner is recommended.

  • Are your products environmentally friendly?

    We strive to minimize our environmental impact by using eco-friendly materials for artificial fur and adhering to ethical production standards.

  • Are your products suitable for cold climates?

    Yes, both natural and artificial fur provide comfort and warmth even in low temperatures. Our designs are specifically tailored for various weather conditions.

  • Do you offer custom orders?

    Yes, we provide custom-made products tailored to your design and measurements. Contact us to discuss your preferences.

  • Do you deliver abroad?

    Yes, we ship worldwide. Delivery time and cost depend on your location.
